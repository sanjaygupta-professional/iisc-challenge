# BhāratBrain Team Brief

## Register
brand

## Users
Indian professionals (8-10), mixed technical/non-technical, joining a team meeting. They need to understand the project in 15-20 minutes, feel the urgency, and know their task. Ages 25-45, Bangalore and remote Indian cities. Some live in GitHub; most don't.

## Product Purpose
Visual team brief: makes README.md accessible to non-GitHub-native collaborators. Communicates what we're building, why India needs it, where we are, what each person does, when the deadline is. Captures inline section feedback for async collaboration.

## Tone
Urgent, principled, rooted. Scientific weight without academic distance. Indian-first without performance. Mission briefing, not startup pitch.

## Anti-references
- Webflow/SaaS template landing pages
- Startup pitch deck aesthetics (gradient blobs, glassmorphism)
- Government/NGO plain-text site (form-heavy, blue-link ugly)
- Academic paper layout (Times New Roman, no whitespace)
- Generic "AI company" dark purple/teal with hero metrics

## Strategic principles
- Every claim traceable to a verifiable source
- India advantage is the thesis, not a feature
- BhāratBrain is lead candidate under stress-test, not a final verdict
- Deadline May 20, 2026 is real and urgent
- Devanagari script is identity, not decoration
